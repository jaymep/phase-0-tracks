#elevator_robot
determine [current floor number]
determine [desired floor number]
if [current floor number] is less than [desired floor number]
  push up button
else
  push down button
wait for elevator doors to open
walk into elevator
turn 180 degrees
push the button that corresponds to [desired floor number]
wait for elevator doors to close
wait for elevator to reach [desired floor number]
wait for elevator doors to open
walk out of elevator

#sunny_side_up_egg
set frying pan on stove burner
turn stove burner to medium heat
add oil to frying pan
crack open egg
put egg white and egg yolk into frying pan
discard eggshell
until egg white is solid
  put lid on frying pan
  wait three minutes
  remove lid from frying pan
if egg white is solid
  remove egg from pan
  place egg on plate
  add salt to egg
  add pepper to egg

#fold_fitted_sheet
pick up fitted sheet
locate corners created by seams
fold:
  determine longer side
  bring together corners on longer side
  bring together corners on opposite longer side
bring elastic edges to one side of sheet
repeat fold three times or until no longer able
